About Me

“I’m an OutSystems Developer”, it’s getting more familiar to use this sentence many times during a day. Today I wanted to write what I feel and what it means to me.
Before getting into the professional side, I’ll introduce myself first. I’m Marco Arede, born in Portugal and currently living in The Netherlands. I like to play board games, see movies (specially sci-fi ones), build gadgets, meet with friends and travel to other countries. About my technical background, it all started in Lisbon at Instituto Superior Técnico. There I got my master degree in Information Systems and Computer Engineering , specializing in Enterprise Information Systems and Software Engineering. During my study path I also joined the Master course of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Initially I wanted to build hardware related to robots, but later I discovered software had more practical solutions. Recently I’ve completed a Digital Transformation course because I’m interested about not only technological challenges but also the ones related to human factors.
